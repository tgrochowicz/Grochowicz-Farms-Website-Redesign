All Natural Meat

Why our beef?

Beef is an important component of the Food Guide Pyramid because it provides significant amounts of several key nutrients.

  • Zinc— only 3oz provides RDA
  • Iron– easily absorbed “heme”
  • Protein– complete and high quality
  • B vitamins

According to the United States Department of Agriculture, natural meat is “minimally processed and contains no artificial ingredients.” At Grochowicz Farms we go one step further.

We provide the highest quality meat products by raising our animals without the use of growth hormones or the use of antibiotics. Our cattle are raised on pasture and eat grasses. Grains are slowly introduced into the diet in a controlled amount, along with the grasses. By controlling the amount of grain, the animals do not become sick or develop digestion problems that solely grain-fed cattle can encounter. They are not forced to eat the grain as they are in factory farms or feedlots. Strictly grass fed cattle are much less flavorful. We have developed a supplemental grain-based feeding formula to deliver a well-marbled product that has a superior taste. Rest assured, all of the grain that we feed our animals is grown and harvested right here on our family farm. We take pride in raising our animals in a humane and ecologically sustainable manner.

Our meat is processed with quality control procedures at a USDA inspected facility. The beef is dry-aged a minimum of 14 days before it is cut, vacuum sealed and flash frozen to preserve its perfection. Our All Natural Beef is rated PRIME by the USDA Inspectors.Our ground beef is 92% lean and available in one pound packages or preformed patties.You can also find our own All Beef Franks 8 to a pack. Stop in our farm market where we sell only our own farm raised meats. We have several cuts available for purchase during our regular business hours. Enjoy our beef which is sold by the piece or by value priced packages.Ground Beef and the Ground Sirloin Patties are available at a discount when purchasing a large quantity.

We also have our own Country Smoked Bacon available. Large quantities require advance notice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you wheelchair accessible?
We strive to make as many areas as possible wheelchair accessible including our daytime entertaining hayride.. However, we are a working farm so some areas are not.
Can I bring my own food to the farm?
Sorry, but we don't allow outside food or drinks past the entrance.
Can I have a birthday party at Grochowicz Farms?
Yes, birthday parties can take place during general admission on Saturdays and Sundays. We do not provide special accommodations for your party. You need to make prior arrangements with us so we know when you plan on coming. General admission is required of ALL who attend. Please keep in mind that we are only open weather permitting so you need alternative plans in case we close due to bad weather.
Can I bring my dog with us to the farm?
We ask that you leave your pets at home for the safety of our customers and farm animals; however we do allow service animals needed for special assistance.
Are you open during the week for Fall Family Fun Activities?
Although the store is open during the week, the activity area is only open to the general public on the weekends. Groups of 25 or more are accommodated by reservation only during the weekday hours.
When is a less busy time to come?
Though we can make no guarantees, the first couple weekends are typically much slower than the later weekends in October.
Do you have restroom facilities?
Yes, we have portable restrooms available for use.
How can I get a question answered that wasn't answered here?
If you have a question that still isn't answered you can contact us here.
